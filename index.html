01 A welcoming family practice

Healthcare that treats you like a person, not a number.

Simcoe Medical Clinic is a family and walk-in practice in the heart of Oshawa. We care for the whole family, from a child's first checkup to ongoing support for seniors, with a calm and respectful approach at every visit.

You can choose a male or female doctor, register as a new patient, or simply walk in. With a pharmacy and other services on site, more of your care happens in one trusted place.

Checkups & Physicals

Frequently asked questions.

Yes. We are currently accepting new patients of all ages in central Oshawa, including families, children, seniors, and newcomers. You can register by phone or through our online booking.

Yes. We have experienced male and female doctors, and you can request to book with a male or female physician when you make your appointment.

We are located at 115 Simcoe St. South in central Oshawa, Ontario, L1H 4G7, with free patient parking on site.

We are open Monday to Friday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM and Saturday from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM. We are closed on Sunday.

Yes. We welcome walk-in patients during clinic hours. Booking an appointment is recommended to reduce your wait time, but it is not required.

Yes. Oshawa People Pharmacy is located inside the clinic, so you can fill prescriptions right after your appointment without a second trip.
